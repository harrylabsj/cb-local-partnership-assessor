## Description: <br>
Evaluate overseas distributors, agencies, resellers, and ecosystem partners in minutes. Get weighted scorecards, due-diligence checklists, commercial model comparisons, and pilot plans with exit guardrails -- not generic advice, but ready-to-use assessment frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, business development teams, channel managers, and international expansion leaders use this skill to evaluate overseas distributors, agencies, resellers, influencers, ecosystem partners, and other local go-to-market partners. It produces structured scorecards, due-diligence questions, commercial model comparisons, pilot plans, exit guardrails, and validation tasks for manual review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may treat partnership assessment output as legal, financial, tax, contract, or anti-corruption due diligence. <br>
Mitigation: Present the output as business-planning guidance only and require qualified professional review before signing agreements or acting on regulated obligations. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags include financial-authority and can-make-purchases even though the reviewed package is descriptive and non-executable. <br>
Mitigation: Do not grant tools, credentials, payment authority, network access, or external-action permissions based on this skill; use it only to produce planning artifacts. <br>
Risk: Market, cultural, partner-reputation, and compliance assumptions can be incomplete, outdated, or overgeneralized. <br>
Mitigation: Require local validation, reliable current sources, and explicit evidence gaps before relying on partner scores, red flags, or market adaptation notes. <br>
